Class: Recipe

The Recipe class organizes culinary creations based on the type of meal or course. Its subclasses include:

- Breakfast: (e.g. Pancakes, MilkAndCerial)

- Lunch: (e.g. Sushi, PizzaMargherita)

- Dinner: (e.g. Carbonara, ChickenCurryWithRice)

- Dessert: (e.g. CarrotCake, ChocolateCake)

- Salad: (e.g. Ceasars, GreekSalad)

This classification allows for easy retrieval and association of recipes based on meal timing or type, aiding in meal planning and dietary analysis within the ontology.
