Class: Food

The Food class encompasses all edible items and is organized into several key subclasses to reflect different food categories:

- Dairy (e.g., milk, cheese)
- Grain (e.g., rice, oats)
- Liquid (e.g., water, oil)
- Meat (e.g., beef, chicken)
- Nutrient: Further divided into Additives, Carbs, Fats, and Vitamins, allowing for detailed nutritional information.
- Pasta (e.g., spaghetti, macaroni)
- Seafood (e.g., fish, shrimp)
- Sweets (e.g., sugar, chocolate)
- VegetableOrFruit (e.g., carrots, apples)
